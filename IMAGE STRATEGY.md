## **4\. Visual Restrictions & Guidelines**

# **To maintain visual consistency and brand integrity as RIT Energy Limited grows, adhere strictly to these constraints:**

### **DO NOT USE:**

* # **Generic stock photos.**

* # **Overly staged business scenes (e.g., people pointing at a blank monitor).**

* # **Standard handshake imagery.**

* # **Artificial-looking "corporate smiles" or exaggerated poses.**

* # **Bright, sterile, typical generic office lighting.**

### **ALWAYS ENFORCE:**

* # **Authenticity: Capture moments of real process or genuine connection.**

* # **Cultural Context: Imagery should reflect modern African infrastructure, innovation, and leadership.**

* # **Complexity: Show the intricate engineering, real data, and high-tech environments that "power industries."**

* # **Structured Organization: Even in workshop or event photos, the environment must feel curated and purposeful.**
